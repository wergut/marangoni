Our teaching methodologies

At Istituto Marangoni Miami, we take pride in our personalized approach to education, placing our students at the heart of their learning journey. Our commitment to providing a real-life, hands-on educational experience sets the stage for success in the dynamic and competitive fashion industry. Our courses are taught by adjunct professors who are not only experts in their fields but also actively working in the industry. They bring real-life scenarios, insights, and the latest industry trends to the classroom, ensuring that students are well-prepared to navigate the ever-evolving fashion landscape.

We believe in a sustainable approach to teaching, instilling in our students the importance of seeking innovative ways to contribute to a more equitable, visually captivating, and well-designed world. It's not just about fashion; it's about designing the future. At Istituto Marangoni Miami, we empower our students to be agents of change, equipping them with the knowledge and skills to make a positive impact in the global design community. Join us in designing the future where creativity, sustainability, innovation, and commitment set the stage.
