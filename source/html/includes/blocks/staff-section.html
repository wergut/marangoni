Dean of Fashion

Keanan Duffty serves as the Dean of Fashion at Istituto Marangoni Miami. He is an award-winning British fashion designer and the author of Rebel Rebel: Anti-Style (Rizzoli/Universe, 2009). Duffty earned a Bachelor of Arts degree with 1st Class Honors in fashion from Central Saint Martins, London. In 2007 Duffty became a member of the Council of Fashion Designers of America (CFDA) and his collections have been shown at New York Fashion week from 1998-2010.
