Designing the future
Research Innovation: trend forecasting, critical thinking

1200 With the Miami campus initiated in 2018 and located in the Design District, studying a Bachelor’s of Fine Arts fashion degree at Istituto Marangoni Miami grants students the chance to succeed in the most in-demand fashion careers. Even in the United States, Istituto Marangoni Miami maintains its European foundation by teaching the Italian way of design and fashion but with a twist of the Miami style. IMM students are encouraged to complete their associate degree to then move on to their bachelor’s degree, and have the opportunity for acceptance into a Marangoni master’s program if desired. Once participants complete their degree, they transition into the Istituto Marangoni alumni group alongside major fashion names like Franco Moschino, Domenico Dolce, and Alessandro Sartori.

The fashion institute supports its students with a close-knit community, high quality fashion technology, immersive classrooms stocked with materials, and a network of talented and accomplished peers, alumni, and faculty for guidance through the educational journey at Marangoni Miami and into the world of fashion and luxury brands.
