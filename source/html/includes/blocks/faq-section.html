What is a bachelor’s degree in fashion?

A Bachelor’s Degree in fashion or a Bachelor’s in Fine Arts degree from Istituto Marangoni Miami is a 4-year program established to implement all of the teachings needed in the fashion industry in order for students to succeed once they graduate and are in the real-world of fashion. IMM offers various branches in the fashion bachelor’s degree to choose from based on the applicant's interests and aspirations. A BFA in Fashion Design teaches students the knowledge and skills needed for designing clothing and accessories – pattern cutting, garment technology, etc. all while developing the student’s personal style.

A BFA in Fashion Styling introduces concepts in visual communication, trend forecasting, and the analysis of style and brands to apply to ever changing technologies in the industry. A BFA in Fashion Business highlights the different methods and business types that are part of the global fashion industry through the aspects of management, marketing, and practices.
